Game Rules

  1. The person playing the red pieces go first. Players will talk alternating turns moving across the board.
  2. The pieces always move diagonally and single pieces can only move forward one square.
  3. To capture an opponent's piece, your piece will skip over theirs in a diagonal straight line. The landing square must be empty and the opponent will tale their piece off the board.
  4. Multiple jumps over an opponent can be made in a single turn.
  5. When a piece makes it over to the other side of the board, they are upgraded to a king. Stack two piece together to take a tall king.
  6. Kings can move diagonally in whatever direction that choose to capture an opponent's piece
  7. A player wins when the opponent cannot make a move or their opponent's pieces have all been captured.
